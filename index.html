Higher Level Programming

Simon Funke <simon@simula.no>


Latest version - August 2017

This lecture series introduces concepts of higher level programming. The lecture introduces essential tools to quickly and efficiently implement programming problems.

The lecture was initially created by Hans Petter Langtangen and extended by Joakim Sundnes, Ola Skavhaug, Jonathan Feinberg, Karl-Erik Holter, Vidar Tonaas Fauske, Benjamin Ragan-Kelley and Simon Funke. It is being taught on a yearly basis at University of Oslo under the name INF3331/INF4331.
