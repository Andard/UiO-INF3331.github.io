Higher Level Programming

2 day crash-course

Simon Funke <simon@simula.no>, Vidar Fauske <vidar@simula.no>


Latest version - January 2017

This lecture series introduces concepts of higher level programming. The lecture introduces essential tools to quickly and efficiently implement programming problems.

The lecture was created initially by Hans Petter Langtangen continued and extended by Joakim Sundnes, Ola Skavhaug, Jonathan Feinberg and Simon Funke. It is being taught on a yearly basis at University of Oslo under the name INF3331/INF4331.
